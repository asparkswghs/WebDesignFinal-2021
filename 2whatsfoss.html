What's FOSS?

The terms 'FOSS' stands for 'Free, Open Source Software'. Though FOSS is inevitably free of charge, the 'Free' in FOSS means Free as in Freedom, or Libre. The freedom to use your PC in the way you want, and be able to accomplish whatever you want.
